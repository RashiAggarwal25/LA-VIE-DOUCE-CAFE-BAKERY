Our Roots

Our Story

La Vie Douce (meaning "The Sweet Life") was born from a simple idea—to create a curated lifestyle space where people can enjoy exceptional artisan coffee, exquisitely beautiful desserts, and deeply memorable hospitality.

Inspired by the elegant European street cafes of Milan and Paris, we have fused traditional baking methods and premium beans with contemporary interiors to design a chic sanctuary in Gurugram.

"We don't believe in rushed mornings. We believe in slow sips, rich conversations, and pastry crumbs on premium tables."

Milestones

Our Journey

2019

The Seed of An Idea

Our founders spent months travelling through European coffee hubs, gathering inspiration, learning culinary methodologies, and roasting nuances before creating the brand framework.

2020

The Debut in Delhi NCR

Opening our doors amidst unprecedented times, offering slow-living comfort and fresh artisan croissants that captured coffee lovers' hearts.

2022

Curating the Signature space

Expanding into our flagship Gurugram experience center, boasting bespoke marble bar designs, customized roasting equipment, and botanical architecture.

2026

Continued Excellence

Refining our craft every single day, training top baristas, introducing premium seasonal menus, and consistently setting standards for fine lifestyle baking.
